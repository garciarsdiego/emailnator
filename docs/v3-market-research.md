# Emailnator V3 market research

## Positioning read

Emailnator should not compete as a full email service provider in V3. Klaviyo, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Brevo and GetResponse already own the broad promise of CRM, automation, segmentation, sending and analytics. The stronger opening is narrower and sharper: an AI campaign studio that turns brand context into editable emails, sequences and export-ready HTML.

## Competitive groups

### Full suites

- Klaviyo: strongest benchmark for AI connected to customer data, campaign creation, flows, send-time optimization and omnichannel marketing.
- Mailchimp: broad small-business suite with templates, automation, AI support and ecommerce integrations.
- HubSpot: CRM-centered email creation and automation for teams already inside its ecosystem.
- Brevo and GetResponse: value-oriented suites that combine email, automation and landing forms.

Lesson: do not imply native sending, CRM or analytics unless those capabilities exist. Use export and campaign creation as the honest V3 promise.

### Standalone builders

- Stripo: drag-and-drop email creation, reusable modules, HTML/CSS workflows, previews and export to many ESPs.
- Beefree / RGE Studio: collaborative creation, approval and export workflows.
- Unlayer: embeddable builder, AI assistant, templates, assets and export APIs.

Lesson: the editor must feel production-grade. Export is not a side feature; it is a core trust point.

### Creator and SaaS-native email tools

- beehiiv: newsletter-first writing and growth workflows.
- Loops: simple SaaS email platform for product, marketing and transactional email.

Lesson: make the path from idea to useful message short. Avoid enterprise heaviness.

## Product opportunity

V3 should own this phrase internally:

> From idea to email, with brand context intact.

Core differentiators:

- brand memory before generation;
- guided campaign briefing instead of raw prompts;
- comparable alternatives for subject, preheader, CTA and body;
- visual block editing after generation;
- funnel sequences for 3 to 5 emails;
- HTML export that does not require native sending.

## Claims allowed without fake proof

Use:

- Create campaigns with brand context.
- Generate emails and sequences in Portuguese.
- Edit by blocks and export HTML.
- Keep emails, templates and funnels in one workspace.
- Use your own sending platform.

Avoid unless proven:

- trusted by named brands;
- numerical conversion gains;
- number of users, emails, revenue or savings;
- deliverability guarantees;
- native ESP integrations that are not implemented.

## Naming options

Keep `Emailnator` for V3 unless the product is being prepared for a public brand relaunch. It is memorable enough and already wired through the repo. Suggested product-line language:

- Emailnator Studio
- Emailnator Campaign Studio
- Emailnator V3, internal only
- Emailnator, with public tagline "campanhas com contexto"

Recommended public name: `Emailnator`.
Recommended product descriptor: `Campaign Studio`.

## Target audience

The user approved a broad audience: ecommerce, creators, agencies, small businesses and SaaS founders. V3 should not list all of them in the hero. Use a broader, behavior-based target:

> Times que precisam transformar intenção, oferta e contexto em campanhas prontas para editar.

Examples can rotate through ecommerce, creator launches, agency retainers and SaaS lifecycle emails inside sections or templates.

## Landing architecture

1. Hero: campaigns with context, product editor visible.
2. Briefing: brand memory and guided campaign setup.
3. Features: memory, alternatives, editor, funnels, HTML export.
4. Editor showcase: block editing, preview, save and export.
5. Funnel sequence: multi-email journey with timing and conditions.
6. Pricing / final CTA: honest promise, no native sending claim.

## Pricing copy direction

Plans should be explained by useful production capacity, not vague AI access.

- Free: explore the workflow and create first drafts.
- Creator: recurring campaigns and exports for one brand.
- Studio: agencies, multiple brands and higher usage.
- Credits: extra campaign generation or analysis when needed.

Every plan should state what is included in plain terms: emails, site analyses, funnels, saved documents and export.
